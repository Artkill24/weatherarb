Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

WeatherArb collects: email addresses (newsletter signups only), anonymous usage analytics via Cloudflare, and API request logs (IP, timestamp, endpoint) for rate limiting purposes.

2. How We Use Data

Email addresses are used only to send weather anomaly alerts and newsletters. We never sell personal data to third parties. API logs are retained for 30 days for abuse prevention.

3. Cookies

We use essential cookies for site functionality. Google AdSense uses cookies for advertising. You can opt out via Google's ad settings. We use no tracking cookies beyond AdSense.

4. Google AdSense

This site uses Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visit. See Google Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

EU users have the right to access, correct, or delete their data. To exercise these rights, contact privacy@weatherarb.com. We comply with GDPR and Italian data protection law.

6. Data Retention

Newsletter emails are retained until unsubscription. API logs: 30 days. Analytics: 12 months aggregated only.

7. Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@weatherarb.com
